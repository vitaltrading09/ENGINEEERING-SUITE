Notes: Transformer testing aligns with the 8 routine tests required by SANS 780 (winding resistance, voltage ratio & polarity/vector group, impedance voltage & load loss, no-load loss & current, induced overvoltage withstand, separate source withstand, paint thickness, effectiveness of sealing). IR (Megger) tests are Transformco WP 31 pre-tests and not a SANS 780 routine test, but are mandatory prior to HV withstand testing. Any failure must be reported immediately to the Designer, Internal Sales Manager or Winding Foreman. Safety prerequisites (LOTO, earthing, PPE, residual charge discharge) must be confirmed before any test equipment is connected.
